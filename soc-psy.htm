Psychology

Last updated: 30 April 2000

  • Centre for Critical Psychology at University of Western Sydney (Australia) - "to conduct leading edge research in critical psychology and cultural theory/studies, to provide research and support in a local, national and international context, and to develop ways to understand and work with critical psychologies and theories of subjectivity"

  • Critical psychology - Dennis Fox's web-site providing access to lots of relevant resources

  • International Society for Theoretical Psychology - "an international platform for theoretical and meta-theoretical analysis in psychology. Its focus is on the contemporary psychological debates. Founded in 1985 in Plymouth, Great Britain, it aims to stimulate theoretical debate, to innovate theory in the discipline, and to serve an integrating role. It also tries to serve as the stage for the discussion for new theoretical ideas and conceptual frameworks, for posing new problems with psychological theories, and for discussions concerning the relation of theoretical psychology with the history of psychology and the philosophy of science"

  • Kritische Psychologie - a German web-site focussing on critical psychology as developed by Klaus Holzkamp (1927-1995) and his colleagues at the Institute for Critical Psychology, Free University Berlin (Germany) (only available in German)

  • Psychoanalytic Resources on the WWW - a pretty comprehensive meta-site offering access to WWW resources, provided by Parfen Laszig (University of Heidelberg, Germany)

  • Psychology Resources - the best meta-site I know of offering access to psychology resources on the web - check this out

  • PsycPort - news and other resources from the world of psychology presented by the American Psychological Association

  • PsychWeb - thousands of links to psychology resources

    • Scholarly psychology resources

  • Radical Psychology Network

  • Scott Oddies Psychology Web-Site - this is a beauty as regards traditional psychology with a critical touch

  • Sigmund Freud Museum Vienna (Austria)

    • Sigmund Freud: Conflict and Culture - an exhibition by the Library of Congress (USA)

  • Society for the Psychological Study of Social Issues - "an international group of over 3500 psychologists, allied scientists, students, and others who share a common interest in research on the psychological aspects of important social issues. In various ways, the Society seeks to bring theory and practice into focus on human problems of the group, the community, and nations, as well as the increasingly important problems that have no national boundaries"

  • Today in the History of Psychology

  • International Journal of Psychoanalysis
