Bibliographies

The following links lead to books and journal articles which I feel are of interest with regard to their respective subjects. In these times of almost unlimited access to the universe of information it seems to be pretty idiosyncratic to provide bibliographies on the Internet. I feel, however, that quantity does not necessarily match quality, and I certainly don't believe that my subjective selection of publications relevant to me does any harm to anybody else.

I will update these bibliographies regularly because they are taken from my own database. As soon as I find something new which attracts my mind, I will not only put it into my database but also onto the Internet.
