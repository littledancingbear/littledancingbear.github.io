Positions held:


1995 - 1998: Lecturer, Griffith University, Faculty of Environmental Sciences and subsequently School of Public Health

1994 - 1995 : President, Management Committee of Training Associates Philippines (TAP), Manila - training in human resource development, training of trainers, teambuilding, organizational change, worksite health promotion

1993 - 1995 : Consultant to the University of the Philippines, School of Public Health (Manila): Training course in health promotion (lectures in principles and strategies of health promotion)

1991 : Consultant to the Commission of the European Communities (Luxembourg): EEC-Network of projects in drug prevention for youth with special emphasis on AIDS related problems: establishing collaboration between projects from 6 Member States, developing a program of work, representation of the EEC at international conferences, presenting keynote addresses at several international and national conferences on HIV/AIDS prevention for youth (10 months)

1990 - 1991 : Consultant to the World Health Organization, Global Program on AIDS (Geneva, Switzerland): AIDS Health Promotion for youth. Review of existing programmatic experiences: global survey on projects, installation of a database on addresses, projects, and literature, guide for AIDS health promotion for youth, representation of WHO at international conferences, presenting keynote addresses at several international and national conferences on HIV/AIDS prevention for youth (12 months)

1989 : Consultant to the Institute for Medical Cooperation (Cologne, Germany): Community participation in water and sanitation projects in developing countries (collaboration with the International Reference Centre for Water and Sanitation, The Hague, Netherlands, and GTZ , Germany - German Society for Technical Cooperation, the leading German foreign aid agency: guide on community participation in water and sanitation projects (6 months)

1987 : Consultant to the Federal Center for Health Education (Cologne, Germany), Worksite health promotion. Documentation of projects and proposals for enterprises: Development of a literature database, program development, guide for worksite health promotion, representation of the Federal Center for Health Education at several international and national conferences, presenting keynote addresses on worksite health promotion (12 months)

1986 : Consultant to the Institute for Medical Cooperation (Cologne, Germany): Health promotion related to water and sanitation projects in developing countries (cooperation with medical professionals and engineers) (12 months)

1985 : Consultant to the Commission of the European Communities, Directorate Labour and Safety (Luxembourg): Health education in schools (organization of an international governmental meeting, preparation of background papers, preparation of final report) (6 months)

1984 : Consultant to the Ministry of Labor, Environment and Social Affairs of the State of Hessen (Wiesbaden, Germany): Development of a state program on health promotion (3 months)

1984 - 1985 : Consultant to UNESCO (Paris, France): Health Workshops for Youth (a participatory approach to health promotion for youth, design of a curriculum, teaching strategies, evaluation methods, representation of UNESCO at several international and national conferences, presentation of keynote addresses on health promotion for youth) (10 months)

1984 : Consultant to World Health Organization, Headquarters, Clearinghouse on Smoking and Health (Geneva, Switzerland): Smoking and health. A cultural approach to an educational programme (developing of a health education program, organization of an international meeting, preparation of background documents and final report) (3 months)

1982 - 1984 : Consultant to the German-French Youth Office (Bad Honnef, Germany): Cultural differences in dealing with one's body in everyday life (facilitation of cross-cultural learning among youth from France, Germany and Italy, developing a training program, representation of the German-French Youth Office at international and national conferences, preparation of final report) (10 months)

1982 : Consultant to the European Centre for Social Welfare Training and Research (Vienna, Austria): Youth, everyday life and health. Lifestyles of youth in big city areas (organization of an international seminar in relation with WHO-EURO involving experts, youth representatives, politicians, facilitation of cross-cultural learning, representation of the European Centre for Social Welfare, Training and Research at international conferences) (4 months)

1982 : Consultant to the Federal Center for Health Education (Cologne, Germany) in collaboration with the World Health Organization, Regional Office for Europe (Copenhagen, Denmark): Lifestyles and living conditions and their impact on health (organization of an international conference involving politicians and experts from Europe and the U.S.A, representation of WHO at international and national conferences, presentation of keynote addresses on lifestyles and living conditions an their impact on health) (4 months)

1980 - 1981 : Consultant and from January 1981, Deputy Principal Investigator of the evaluation study of the German Cardiovascular Prevention Study at the University of Freiburg (Germany), Department of Medical Sociology: head of an interdisciplinary group of professionals; design of the evaluation study; negotiations with the intervention centers; negotiations with the governmental funding agencies; presentation of the project at international and national conferences, presentation of invited papers

1978 - 1981 : Academic Assistant (lecturer and research fellow) at the Technical University of Berlin; teaching in socialization theory and research, sociology of youth, sociology of youth subcultures, including field visits and ethnographic interviews with youth

1975 - 1976 : Lecturer in media didactics/educational technology at the University of Bremen: training of teachers for vocational training (electricians and electronic engineers) regarding the educational/training component of their future positions; each course was attended by 20-25 students

1974 - 1976 : Lecturer in media didactics at the Free University of Berlin: training of students in the use of media in the context of training inside and outside of schools; each course was attended by 15-20 students

1973 - 1978 : Researcher at the Federal Institute for Vocational Research, Department of Media Research (Berlin); country-wide demonstration project on the implementation of educational technology in the training of electricians and electronic engineers; collaboration with teachers, trainers, engineers, media specialists; social research and evaluation regarding the implementation process, its impact on social relations among students and teachers/trainers, and organizational change in institutions of vocational training.
