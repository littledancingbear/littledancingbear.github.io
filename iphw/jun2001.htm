Archives: June 2001

Last updated: 6 July 2001






  • 29 June 2001:
    The International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies has published the "World Disaster Report 2001", which is made available online at:
    http://www.ifrc.org/publicat/wdr2001/

  • 26 June 2001:
    Human Rights Watch has published the report "In the shadow of death: HIV/AIDS and children's rights in Kenya", which is made available online at:
    http://www.hrw.org/reports/2001/kenya/

  • 23 June 2001:
    UNDP has published a new policy paper on "HIV/AIDS - Implications for poverty reduction", which is made available online as a PDF-file (roughly 485KB) at:
    http://www.undp.org/dpa/frontpagearchive/2001/june/22june01/hiv-aids.pdf

  • 16 June 2001:
    The WHO Regional Office for the Americas (PAHO) has published a report on "Equity in health. From an Ethnic Perspective", which is made available as a PDF-file (roughly 6.4MB !!) at:
    http://www.paho.org/English/HDP/HDD/etnia.pdf

  • 16 June 2001:
    UNEP has published the report "GLOBIO - Global Methodology for mapping human impact on the biosphere", which is made available online at:
    http://www.grida.no/prog/polar/globio/rovaniemi.htm

  • 14 June 2001:
    The Australian Institute for Health and Welfare (AIHW) has published the report "Statistics on Drug Use in Australia (2000)", which is made available online at:
    http://www.aihw.gov.au/publications/phe/sdua00/index.html

  • 5 June 2001:
    The United Nations Center for Human Settlements (Habitat) has published the report "State of the World's Cities Report 2001", which is made available online as a series of PDF-files at:
    http://www.unchs.org/Istanbul+5/statereport.htm
