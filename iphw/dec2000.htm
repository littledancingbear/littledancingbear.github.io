Archives: December 2000

Last updated: 1 January 2001






  • 29 December 2000:
    The World Resources Institute has published the report "Pilot Analysis of Global Ecosystems (PAGE): Forest ecosystems", which "reveals that forest areas in developed countries continue to increase slightly, while clearance for agriculture, development, and logging in developing countries are reducing their forests by at least 140,000 square kilometers every year.". The report is available at:
    http://www.wri.org/wri/wr2000/forests_page.html

  • 24 December 2000:
    The UN Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) offers access to reports and databases of the "Global Information and Early Warning System on Food and Agriculture (GIEWS)" at the following URL:
    http://www.fao.org/WAICENT/faoinfo/economic/giews/english/giewse.htm

  • 21 December 2000:
    The International Labor Organization (ILO) has published the report "HIV/AIDS in Africa: The impact on the world of work", which is available as a PDF-file (roughly 660KB) at:
    http://www.ilo.org/public/english/protection/trav/aids/pdf/adftext.pdf

  • 20 December 2000:
    The European Environment Agency has published the report "Down to earth: Soil degradation and sustainable development in Europe. A challenge for the 21st century", which is made available as a PDF-file (roughly 1.25MB) at:
    http://themes.eea.eu.int/toc.php/state/soil?doc=39303&l=en

  • 20 December 2000:
    The World Bank, WHO, UNICEF, UNAIDS, UNESCO and UNFPA have published a report on "Health, a key to Prosperity: Success Stories in Developing Countries", which is made available online as a series of PDF-files at:
    http://wbln0018.worldbank.org/HDNet/HDdocs.nsf/9fdfa192251170fc852568080054ac29/ccb353ef69b39683852569ba00559680?OpenDocument

  • 15 December 2000:
    The National Family Health Survey of India has published the results of the 1998-1999 studies online at:
    http://www.nfhsindia.org/publi.html

  • 14 December 2000:
    The International Council on Human Rights Policy has published a report on "Local perspectives: foreign aid to the justice sector", which is available online as a PDF-file (roughly 430KB) at:
    http://www.ichrp.org/excerpts/12.pdf

  • 14 December 2000:
    The Government of the United Kingdom has published a policy paper "Eliminating World Poverty: Making Globalisation Work for the Poor", which is made available online at:
    http://www.globalisation.gov.uk/

  • 13 December 2000:
    UNICEF has published the report "State of the World's Children 2001", which is made available online at:
    http://www.unicef.org/sowc01/

  • 9 December 2000:
    The Council of Europe publishes a newsletter on "Social Cohesion: Developments" three times a year. The first issue, November 2000, is now available as a PDF-file (roughly 140KB) at:
    http://www.coe.fr/DASE/EN/cohesion/strategy/devunit.htm

  • 9 December 2000:
    The Carnegie Council on Ethics and International Affairs offers free subscriptions to a range of mailing lists on human rights, international development and other topics. The lists are accessible at:
    http://www.cceia.org/publications/newsletter.html

  • 8 December 2000:
    The Human Rights Watch World Report 2001 has been published online at:
    http://www.hrw.org/wr2k1/

  • 6 December 2000:
    The Pesticides Residues Committee (UK) has published its report for the first quarter of 2000, which is available online at:
    http://www.pesticides.gov.uk/committees/PRC/prc.htm

  • 6 December 2000:
    Equity in Health - a web-site provided by the International Health Program of the Department of Health Services at the School of Public Health and Community Medicine in the University of Washington, Seattle, USA and Health Alliance International, Seattle, USA

  • 6 December 2000:
    The David Rockefeller Center for Latin American Studies (DRCLAS) at Harvard University produced a newsletter on "Health and Equity in the Americas", which is available online at:
    http://www.fas.harvard.edu/~drclas/pages/tabpages/publications/newsletters/fall00/

  • 6 December 2000:
    The World Bank has published its annual report "Global Economic Prospects and the Developing Countries 2001", which is made available at:
    http://www.worldbank.org/prospects/gep2001/

  • 3 December 2000:
    "The Canadian Sustainability Report provides Canada's first independent assessment of how we are doing at living in balance for the long term."
    http://www.sustreport.org/

  • 3 December 2000:
    The US National Library of Medicine (NLM) has recently placed online over 5,000 abstracts from the XIII International AIDS Conference held in Durban, South Africa, July 9-14, 2000. Users can search by abstract ID, author, title, or text. Unfortunately, the collection is not browseable.
    http://www.iac2000.org
