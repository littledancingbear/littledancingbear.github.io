AMRO/PAHO Members

 

  • General Remarks

    • The WHO Regional Office for the Americas (AMRO) / the Pan American Health Organization (PAHO) is located in Washington, D.C. ( U.S.A. ).

    • AMRO/PAHO comprises of countries from Northern, Central, and Southern America.
