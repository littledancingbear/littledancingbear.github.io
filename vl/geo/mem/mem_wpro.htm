WPRO Members & Territories

 

  • General Remarks

    • The WHO Regional Office for the Western Pacific (WPRO) is located in Manila ( Philippines ).

    • WPRO comprises of 36 countries and territories of Australasia, though some states decided to join other Regional Offices for political, cultural, or confessional reason.
