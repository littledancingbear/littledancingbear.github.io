AFRO Members

  • General Remarks

    • The WHO Regional Office for the Africa (AFRO) is located in Harare ( Zimbabwe ).

    • Not all countries of the African Continent are members of AFRO. Most of the Northern African countries have joined the WHO Regional Office for the Eastern Mediterranean, which is located in Alexandria (Egypt).
