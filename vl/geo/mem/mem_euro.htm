EURO Members

 

  • General Remarks

    • The WHO Regional Office for Europe (EURO) is located in Copenhagen ( Denmark ).

    • EURO comprises of 51 countries, mainly of Europe. Some states and territories from Asia, the Middle East, and Oceania have decided to join EURO for political, cultural, or confessional reason.
