"Our objectives are - to provide support and assistance to persons living with HIV and AIDS (PWAs); to increase awareness, education and understanding of AIDS and HIV infection; to combat discrimination and stigmatisation of (PWAs) and their loved ones; and to encourage AIDS-related research activities in Singapore"
    • Care of the Elderly Foundation - "founded in 1995 to provide a total series of community care services to the less fortunate and the needy elderly. It is a non-profit welfare charitable organisation and a full member with the National Council of Social Service (NCSS)"

    • ENGENDER - Centre for Environment, Gender and Development. "We are an independent organisation with a non-profit philosophy, working in Asia. Our vision is to engender sustainable communities in a region where livelihoods, health and habitats have been critically affected by rapid developmental changes"
