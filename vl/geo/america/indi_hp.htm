  • Publications

    • Health of Native People of North America: An Annotated Mediagraphy, Part 1 and 2 - excellent resource on media productions concerning health issues of American Indians

    • Statement of the American Dietetic Association On the FY 1998 Appropriations Legislation For The Indian Health Service

    • Changing Numbers, Changing Needs: American Indian Demography and Public Health (1996) - provided by National Academic Press, you can read large parts of the book, however the loading time per page is pretty high. Check it out yourself

    • Health Status Profile of American Indians in Arizona: 1995 Data Book - provided by the Arizona Center for Health Statistics

    • The State of Native American Youth Health - full-text online report commissioned by the Indian Health Service in 1992

    • Native Americans and Traffic Safety

    • Indian Health Care. A report by the U.S. Congress, Office of Technology Assessment, from 1986. - this link provides access to the entire report
