Topic's Information

 

  • Circle of friends - The Circle of Friends is part of the American Paralysis Association (APA), a non-profit organization founded in 1982 to encourage and support research to find a cure for paralysis caused by spinal cord injury and other central nervous system disorders.

  • Deaf Health Network - "to provide information, materials, and resources related to the Deaf/Hard of Hearing and Health Care communities. DHN also seeks to create a forum for health care and social work professionals to network with other professionals and find information and resources (professional development and/or research)"

  • Disability Organizations Network - "to assist organizations both of and for persons with disabilities in their search for partner organizations for joint projects; events; sharing resource persons for lectures; training or technical assistance; for study visits, internships or other cooperation, including funding"

  • Disability-Related Sites on the Web

  • Disability Resource Center - provides a number of resources on disability organizations, services and it includes links to www-sites by disabled persons

  • Disability Resources on the Internet - large collection of links to almost all aspects of disabilities

  • Office of Disability (USA)

  • VICNET Disability Page - rich resources on the subject regarding Australia and the WWW

  • WHO Program for the Prevention of Blindness and Deafness
