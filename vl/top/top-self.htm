Topic's Information

 

  • American Self-Help Clearinghouse - a comprehensive source book online covering the USA and other countries

  • Emotional Support on the Internet via Newsgroups - a list of more than 200 newsgroups including their access addresses which provide support to everyone with regard to their specific topic

  • National Self-Help Clearinghouse (USA) - "a not-for-profit organization that was founded in 1976 to facilitate access to self-help groups and increase the awareness of the importance of mutual support"

  • Personal Empowerment Page - "a list of American web-based resources provided for those who are searching for advice, informational resources, or just the knowledge that there are others out there in similar situations, and most of the services and informational resources listed here are either low-cost or no-cost"

  • Psychological Self-Help - providing access to a book on self-help written from a psychological perspective

  • Self Help Nottingham (UK) - this excellent web-site offers access to resources on local, national and international level

  • Self-Help Resource Center (Toronto, Canada) - "to promote self-help/mutual aid. The goals of the organization are to increase awareness about self-help/mutual aid in the community and among helping professionals, and to facilitate the growth and development of self-help groups, networks and resources"
