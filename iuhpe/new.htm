  • Previews of public health journals
    Oxford University Press provides a free table of contents "alerting service". Web users can register to receive advance notification of the table of contents for any of the public health journal titles produced by OUP. The list includes Health Education Research, one of IUHPE's official publications. The service is available whether or not you are a regular subscriber to HER or any other OUP public health journal. To sign up for this new service, visit: http://www.oupjournals.org/ and select the journals which interest you.
    Access to a large selection of public health journals is also available with the WWW Virtual Library: Public Health at:
    http://www.ldb.org/vl/top/top-jour.htm

  • "Tuberculosis Treatment for All" Declaration
    On 24 March 2000, World Tuberculosis Day, the International Union Against Tuberculosis and Lung Disease (IUATLD) launched a campaign for TB treatment for all that is: free, adequate and uninterrupted, accessible, and administered by trained health workers. IUHPE's Executive Director Marie-Claude Lamarre has signed the declaration herself and urges others to do the same. Visit: http://www.iuatld.org/html/online_declaration.htm
