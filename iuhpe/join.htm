If you wish to join the IUHPE, here are the membership categories and the annual fees. Please note that conversion rates may change, and the reference currency is the French Franc (FF). Thank you very much for your kind support.

INDIVIDUAL MEMBERSHIP:
French Francs 460 = approximately US$67.00


Annual fee includes one subscription to the quarterly IUHPE-Journal Promotion and Education.

GROUP MEMBERSHIP:
French Francs 1,480 = approximately US$216.00


Annual fee includes three subscriptions to the quarterly IUHPE-Journal Promotion and Education.
