Minimising the harm: Health in prisons - 14-16 February 1999, Sydney (Australia)

The Public Health Association of Australia made a commitment to organise and sponsor a conference on public health issues in correctional facilities because there is a real need for an independent, national evaluation of the standards and policies relevant to health in Australian prisons.

The conference will provide the first national forum in Australia for health professionals, social researchers, custodial staff and human rights activists to share experiences, aspirations and wisdom.
