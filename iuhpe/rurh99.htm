5th National Rural Health Conference


14 - 17 March 1999
Adelaide Convention Centre, Adelaide, South Australia

The theme for this Conference will be 'Leaping the Boundary Fence: Using Evidence and Collaboration to Build Healthier Rural Communities'.

This biennial conference is the premier platform for all people interested in rural and remote health outcomes.

The International Year of Older Persons is being promoted in 1999. The Conference will be covering important issues surrounding the health and lifestyle of older Australians.
