Regional Office for the South West Pacific

Last updated: 8 September 2001






Welcome to the web-site of the International Union for Health Promotion and Education, Regional Office for the South West Pacific. This web-site is maintained by the Regional Director, Dr Rosmarie Erben, who is located in Brisbane (Australia) on behalf of the Vice-President of the IUHPE for the Southwest Pacific Region, Marilyn Wise, who is located in Sydney (Australia) with the Australian Centre for Health Promotion.

The Regional Office was established in 1995 and it represents the most recent addition to the regional offices of IUHPE.

IUHPE is the leading international non-governmental organization of health promotion and health education. Its headquarters are based in Paris (France). The web-site of IUHPE Headquarters offers latest news on global developments plus access to further resources, all of which are made available in the three official languages of IUHPE, i.e. English, French, and Spanish. You are kindly invited to visit this site at:
http://www.iuhpe.org/

IUHPE enjoys official status as a non-governmental organization with the World Health Organization (WHO). It closely collaborates with WHO in all matters covered by the mission statement of IUHPE.

This web-site provides information on the activities of the IUHPE Regional Office for the South West Pacific.
